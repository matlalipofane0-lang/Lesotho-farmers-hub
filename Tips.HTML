Business Tips for Farmers

  • Keep clear records of all sales, purchases, and expenses.
  • Advertise your products regularly using WhatsApp, Facebook, or the Lesotho Hub platform.
  • Grow products that are in high demand in your area or nearby markets.
  • Work with other farmers to buy supplies in bulk and reduce costs.
  • Always aim to provide clean, high-quality, and fresh products.
  • Attend agricultural trainings to learn modern and efficient techniques.
  • Respond quickly and professionally to customers.
  • Always calculate your costs before setting product prices.
